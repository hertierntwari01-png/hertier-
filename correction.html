Delicious Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1 pnd of metea
  • 1 g of cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti for 10–12 minutes.
  2. Chop onions and tomatoes.
  3. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Mix with spaghetti and serve warm.
